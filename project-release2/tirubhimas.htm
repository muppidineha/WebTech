Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5/5)

Tirupathi Bhimas, located in the outskirts of the bustling San Jose, is the perfect place for anyone seeking an authentic, traditional South-Indian meal. In line with its traditional name of Tirupathi, or a popular cultural town in southern India, this restaurant does not miss a beat in providing guests with the complete dining experience. Right from bein served on the traditional banana leaf to being able to order a thali, or a sampler plate of sorts with a little bit of everything, you will feel as if you're enjoying a meal in India. But, if you are interested in something a little smaller or more standaard like other restaurants, Tirupathi Bhimas has got you covered with smaller portioned options and some common favorites like pooris and samosas. The restaurant itself is constantly packed with people, both nearby employees and families, but the staff is caring and very helpful, so one never feels overlooked. Tirupathi Bhimas is a must-try for anyone who's in the Bay!
