I'm sure you've seen (and hopefully tasted!) by now the unique Eastern flavours that are so natural and characteristic of the Bay. But, the people of the Bay Area definitely know how to bring in the classics as well. Whether you're looking for a quiet, aesthetic coffee shop with standard coffees and pastries to spend the afternoon at, or you're looking for a trendy spot full of life and some fun performances, the Bay has got you covered. With places offering everything from basic coffees and french pastries to some unique Asian buns and treats, you will surely find something you love!
