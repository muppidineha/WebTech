Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5/5)

Paradise Biryani Pointe is world-famous chain restaurant specializing in the popular South Indian delicacy - Hyderabadi biriyani. Whether you're a Hyderabadi yourself and looking to get a little taste of home, or you're new to the cuisine altogether, this is the perfect place for you! With a wide assortment of biryanis including chicken dum, mutton, vegetable etc. as well as various naans and delicious curries, there is something here for everyone. They even have an array of unique seafood appetizers that are difficult to find anywhere else, including my favorite Apollo fish. And the best part is, there are locations scattered throughout the Bay! While I have grown up going to the Fremont branch, they also have locations in Santa Clara, Milpitas, and Dublin. With a kind and attentive staff, the latest Tollywood music playing in the background, and of course the delicious food, you are sure to have a wonderful meal!
