Hi! My name is Neha, and I am what you may call a connoiusser of all things food. I have always had a huge sweet tooth since childhood and have been obsessed with anything chocolate. Being from a culturally diverse hub, however, I quickly found that chocolate was just the tip of the iceberg. With various Indian sweet shops and Asian bakeries to my left and restaurants galore to my right, I slowly but surely explored quite possibly every type of cuisine I could get my hands on. And after years of exploring every nook and cranny of the Bay, I think it's safe to say that I may know a thing or two about some of the best places to check out when here.

Fun Facts

  • I have an incredibly high spice tolerance
  • Though I have a huge sweet tooth, I like to stick to the basics
    • Find me a basic German chocolate cake from the grocery chain store Safeway or
      a Chocolate Devotion ice-cream from Coldstone, and I'm happy!
  • As a foodie, I am open to trying anything, but I can sometimes be picky
