Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4/5)

Shalimar Restaurant, located in the Southeast Bay city of Fremont, is an Indian and Pakistani restaurant that has been in the business for years now. With a relatively limited menu and a fairly small physical storefront, you may have your doubts, but the food itself more than makes up for it. With a variety of Lucknowi style biryanis, delicious and juicy kebabs, and arguably some of the best naans and North-Indian style curries you'll find in the Bay, it's no wonder that people from around the Bay come flocking to the restaurant. With comparatively low prices for the Bay and exceptionally fast service, the restaurant is a great option whether you're on the go or have some time to kill. The only drawback is that Shalimar's massive popularity and small location make for a slightly noisy and chaotic ambience. But, if this is something you can look past or if you're open to takeout, then this is one place you don't want to miss!
