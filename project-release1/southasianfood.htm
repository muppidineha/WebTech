The Bay Area is home to perhaps one of the largest Indian populations in the United States. As such, there is plenty to choose from when it comes to Indian dining in the Bay. Whether you are looking for a traditional South Indian thaali complete with dosas and pulihora, or you are craving some spicy Mughlai chicken biryani with some fresh sweet mango lassi, there is definitely a spot at the table for you. Prefer a simple hot chai with some snacks? There's plenty of options for that too. And of course, we've picked out some of our favorites to share with you!
