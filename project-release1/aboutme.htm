About Me

Admirer of sugar, spice, and everything nice since '00
Hi! My name is Neha, and I am what you may call a connoiusser of all things food. I have always had a huge sweet tooth since childhood and have been obsessed with anything chocolate. Being from a culturally diverse hub, however, I quickly found that chocolate was just the tip of the iceberg. With various Indian sweet shops and Asian bakeries to my left and restaurants galore to my right, I slowly but surely explored quite possibly every type of cuisine I could get my hands on. And after years of exploring every nook and cranny of the Bay to explore all that the Bay has to offer, I think it's safe to say that I may know a thing or two about some of the best places to check out when here.
Having grown up in the Bay, I've always had a special place in my heart for all of the unique delicacies that have found a home here. Whether you are seeking savory Asian food or trendy desserts, a large chain restaurant or a quaint hole-in-the-wall place, I've got you covered!
