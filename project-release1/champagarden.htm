Ever heard of the tiny Asian country of Laos? Well Laos may be small, but its cuisine is far from. Champa Garden is a small, hole-in-the-wall Laosian restaurant located in the Warm Springs district of the Southeast Bay city of Fremont. With a tiny physical storefront and cozy atmosphere, you will feel right at home. A family-run restaurant, you will feel that love and care flowing through the restaurant, be it through the friendly staff or the delicious meals. While you'll find your typical Thai offerings on the menu, you will also find special Laosian delicacies including Saigok and Nam Kao. With its unique menu and comfortable atmosphere, there is something for everyone. Due to the pandemic, the restaurant has been permanantly closed for sometime, but its owners are confident that the restaurant will be back in no time. So, if you're interested in exploring a brand new Asian cuisine, stop by Champa Garden!
