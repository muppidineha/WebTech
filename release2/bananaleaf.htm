Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5/5)

Banana Leaf is a trendy Malaysian restaurant located in the outskirts of San Jose. With its delicious food and unique style, the place is always packed with young adults and families alike. This restaurant stands out for more reasons than one, but most exciting is its unique open-flame approach. With the talented chefs cooking on an open flame just inches from where you are seated, you are in for a visual treat! The sizzle as you see and hear juicy meats and seafood get cooked right in front of your eyes is sure to make your mouth water. The restaurant offers an exceptionally large menu ranging from tasty Malaysian staples like Roti Prata as well to unique dishes like Nyonya Shaking Beef. Have kids who need something plain and simple? The restaurant has fun basics for them too, as well as engaging in-restaurant activities. Banana Leaf is a must-try on your next visit to the Bay!
