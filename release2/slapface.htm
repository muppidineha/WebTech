Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4/5)

Seeking a traditional coffeeshop experience? A live stand-up comedy show? Both? Then Slapface Coffee and Tea is the place for you! Centrally located in the downtown area of the Southeast Bay city of Fremont, Slapface has got something for everyone. The menu is very simple with the usual offerings of assorted coffee drinks and basic pastries, but what makes this place stand out is its vibrant ambience. With a new live music performance or stand-up event nearly every day, you are bound to be entertained. But if you're looking for a quiet place to study instead, Slapface also has several study rooms available for use, away from the loud hustle and bustle. The only drawback to this spot is perhaps the constant rush of people which, in turn with the live events, can be a bit much for a coffeeshop. The shop has also had to limit its hours due to the ongoing pandemic. But, if you don't mind this and are looking for a fun and memorable experience, then you must give Slapface a try!
