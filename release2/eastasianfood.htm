When you're in the U.S., your first thoughts when you hear "Asian food" are usually limited to sushi and Chinese takeout. But when you step foot in the Bay, these offerings are just the tip of the iceberg. Sure, you'll definitely find your fair share of delicious sushi and Thai food. But what the Bay does differently is that no corner of East Asia goes untouched. Whether you're looking to have a fancy Malaysian dinner or enjoy a casual Laosian dining experience, your options in the Bay are unlimited!
